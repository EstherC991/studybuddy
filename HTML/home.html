Our Goal

StudyBuddy's goal is to provide students around the Union County College campus a simple and fast way to find the help they need. Have you needed to find a tutor but never knew where they were at? Need a specific tutor but don't know their hours? StudyBuddy makes this easier for you to find the solutions to these problems.

Beyond tutoring, the website also facilitates access to practice exams, allows you to connect with classmates, form study groups, and provides a platform to share resources with your peers.

How can I get started?

Using your UCNJ ID you can access Study Buddy to find the help you need! Be sure to provide any feedback if you find an issue or have a suggestion!
